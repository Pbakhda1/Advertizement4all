What you can make

  • Sale ads (discounts, promo codes)
  • Event flyers (date/time/location)
  • Hiring ads (job + pay + apply link)
  • Real estate (price + features)
  • Food ads (menu special)
  • App promos (features + CTA)

This is an MVP demo. You can later connect real AI (OpenAI API) to generate unlimited copy styles.
